Christmas

For Christmas this year I decided to do something a little different to express some holiday cheer to my loving family. Instead of the typical hallmark cards we purchase at our local stores, I created a little web app for them.

Each of my family members received a password christmas morning as well as a link to the app. Upon arriving, each of them can click on his or her name and enter the password received that morning. Upon entering they are animated to their christmas card with a loving message.

Gbuilders

Gbuilders LLC is firm experienced in all areas of construction, located on Broadway in the Financial District of NYC. With 30 years in the instrusty, they have completed some easily recognizeable jobs in every sector - Army Bases, data centers, nightclubs, condominiums, and more.

Republic 50

Republic 50 is a locally owned tavern in the center of Long Island City. The chef created an eclectic combination of small plates that durive from an array of cultures from around the world. In a single meal a guest can have Italian Gnocchi, a side of Tuna Tartar, with a craft beer from Mexico.

DND Group

DND Group is a construction management firm that began in New Jersey. With almost 80 years of industry experience under its belt, they've done work on an array of recognizeable landmarks and universities around New York and New Jersey.

With focus on interactions inspired by Material Design, a single-page site with draggable sections was created. The use of circular buttons with svg stroke animations and radial scales that will overlay specified content gives the website a seemingly mobile app feel on mobile devices.
